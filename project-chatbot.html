Student Chatbot

AI-Powered Assistant for Student Productivity & Organization

The Student Chatbot is a conversational assistant designed to help students stay on top of their academic tasks and schedule. It was built as part of a chatbot architecture project, featuring natural language understanding, intent classification, and slot filling.

Key Features

  • add_task: Add assignments, exams, or projects
  • check_upcoming_tasks: Ask what work is due soon
  • get_study_tips: Request advice for studying
  • add_event_to_schedule: Schedule meetings, classes, or reminders
  • check_upcoming_schedule: Check what's coming up on your calendar

The chatbot was tested across a range of example conversations to ensure it could handle user inputs even when information was missing or provided out of order. Required and optional slots were handled using custom logic.

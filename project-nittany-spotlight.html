Nittany Spotlight

UX Research & App Usability Evaluation Project

Nittany Spotlight is an app designed to help users discover food and drink deals as well as events in downtown State College. The goal of the project was to centralize this information into a simple and usable interface.

Link to project: View Nittany Spotlight on Figma →

Main Pages

Top Deals, Drink Deals, Food Deals, and Events are organized with bold icons and clear structure for quick browsing.

Suggesting Edits

Users can type deal suggestions directly into the app and receive a visual confirmation after submitting.

Comment System

Deals and events have public comment threads. Users can write, reply, and see feedback from others in the app.

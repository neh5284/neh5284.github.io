POWR

Lifting Tracker and Workout Management Application

POWR is a lifting tracking application designed to help users log workouts, track progress, and manage training sessions over time. The goal of the project was to create a fitness-focused interface that gives users a simple way to organize training data, review past performance, and navigate between different workout tools in one place.

Link to project: View POWR on GitHub →

Dashboard

The dashboard gives users a quick overview of their lifting progress with profile information, charts, and previous workout summaries. This page was designed to make it easy to review training history and visualize progress at a glance.

Calendar

The calendar page allows users to view workout days across the month and organize future sessions. This helps turn the app into more than just a logging tool by supporting workout planning and schedule awareness.

Workout Logging

The workout logging feature lets users add exercises, sets, reps, and weight information in a structured way. This part of the app focuses on making workout entry simple while still capturing enough detail to support progress tracking over time.
